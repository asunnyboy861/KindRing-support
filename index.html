Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a care recipient?

Go to Settings > Care Recipient > Add Care Recipient. Enter their name, birthday, and any medical notes.

How do I invite family members?

Go to the Family tab and tap the + button to add a new member to your care circle.

How do I set up medication reminders?

Go to the Medications tab and tap the + button. Enter the medication details and schedule.

How does the Emergency SOS work?

Press and hold the SOS button on the Emergency tab. This will alert your care circle members and share your location.

How do I create a handoff report?

Go to the Handoff tab and tap New Report. Fill in the shift details and any important notes for the next caregiver.
